# Visa-Application-Guide

This is a visa application guide chatbot that helps with all your application related concerns. Just ask your doubts and get all your queries solved in less than a minute.

The bot provides guidance, resource links and step by step support to make visa applications less confusing.
Disclaimer: This bot provides general information and is not a substitute for official govt or legal advice.

**Features**
1. Country specific visa guidance
2. document checklist
3. FAQ support
4. Links to official websites of embassies
5. application tips and tricks

**USage**
Users can ask any type of visa related queries like "What are the documents required for a USA B!/B2 visa?" or "Show me the steps for a UK student visa"
